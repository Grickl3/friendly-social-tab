Should Be Friendly

Web developers have nearly universally accepted a cynical and mysanthropic pattern in embracing the involuntary pop-up as a means of permission asset gathering. In producing sqg-opt, I hope to demonstrate that this pattern can be challenged with superior design thinking to the end that site guests will feel more respected and — I believe — be more generous with their granting of permission assets.
